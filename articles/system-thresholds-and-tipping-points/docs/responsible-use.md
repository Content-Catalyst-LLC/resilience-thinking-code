# Responsible Use

This scaffold is not a system-collapse prediction engine.

Do not use it to:

- claim exact tipping points from synthetic data
- rank communities by collapse risk without participatory review
- justify displacement, abandonment, austerity, or coercive intervention
- treat low adaptive capacity as a failure of affected people
- ignore historical injustice, exposure, public investment, or governance responsibility
- replace domain expertise, field evidence, public accountability, or local knowledge
- treat early warning signals as certainty

Use it to support transparent inquiry into how pressure, feedback, disturbance, adaptive capacity, memory, recovery speed, and governance shape threshold risk.
