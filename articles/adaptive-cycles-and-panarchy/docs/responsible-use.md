# Responsible Use

This scaffold is not a collapse-prediction system.

Do not use it to:

- claim exact release timing from synthetic data
- romanticize collapse as innovation
- justify crisis-driven privatization, displacement, austerity, or institutional abandonment
- treat social suffering as a natural phase
- ignore power, colonial history, race, class, gender, property, or political authority
- replace empirical system history, ecological evidence, or public accountability
- rank communities, institutions, ecosystems, or infrastructures without validated evidence

Use it to support transparent inquiry into how growth, conservation, release, reorganization, memory, novelty, rigidity, and cross-scale dynamics shape resilience.
