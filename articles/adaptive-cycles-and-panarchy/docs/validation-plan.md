# Validation Plan

A professional adaptive-cycle or panarchy model should include:

1. **System-boundary validation**  
   Define the system, essential function, scale, spatial extent, temporal horizon, and relevant nested cycles.

2. **Phase-evidence validation**  
   Do not assign phases based on metaphor alone. Use evidence for growth, conservation, release, or reorganization.

3. **Variable validation**  
   Validate potential, connectedness, resilience, rigidity, memory, novelty, disturbance exposure, and cross-scale influence.

4. **Release-threshold validation**  
   Treat release thresholds as scenario assumptions unless empirically calibrated.

5. **Cross-scale validation**  
   Identify whether revolt and remember dynamics are supported by data, history, governance analysis, or ecological evidence.

6. **Justice and power review**  
   In social systems, evaluate who experiences release, who controls reorganization, and whether memory preserves wisdom or injustice.

7. **Uncertainty review**  
   Label all modeled, observed, inferred, and speculative parameters clearly.
