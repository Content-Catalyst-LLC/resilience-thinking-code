# Responsible Use

This scaffold is not a governance certification tool, public-decision automation system, emergency-power justification tool, or institutional ranking tool.

Do not use it to automate public governance decisions, justify unchecked discretion, weaken legal rights, rank institutions without context, replace community participation, certify public legitimacy, bypass Indigenous rights or knowledge protocols, or hide unequal risk behind a single adaptive governance score.

Use it to make assumptions visible, compare strategies transparently, identify uncertainty, strengthen learning systems, improve coordination, and support accountable adaptive governance planning.
