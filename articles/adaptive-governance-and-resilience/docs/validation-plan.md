# Validation Plan

A professional adaptive governance workflow should validate learning capacity, flexibility, coordination, knowledge integration, legitimacy, accountability, equity safeguards, and implementation feasibility.

Important validation sources include policy revision records, after-action implementation trackers, monitoring-system audits, public trust surveys, community participation records, Indigenous and local knowledge protocols, legal review, budget and staffing data, interagency coordination records, equity audits, public complaint data, scenario exercises, emergency drills, and interviews with affected communities and frontline practitioners.

Quantitative indicators should be interpreted with institutional history, public accountability, rights safeguards, local knowledge, Indigenous sovereignty where relevant, and uncertainty-aware decision-making.
