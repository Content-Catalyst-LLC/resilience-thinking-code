# Responsible Use

This scaffold is not an automatic ranking system for communities, ecosystems, institutions, or infrastructure.

Do not use it to:

- rank vulnerable communities without participatory review
- claim precise collapse thresholds from synthetic data
- treat low adaptive capacity as a failure of affected people
- excuse institutional abandonment by praising community resilience
- ignore power, inequality, historical injustice, or public responsibility
- replace domain expertise, public accountability, or local knowledge
- justify disinvestment, displacement, or risk transfer

Use it to support transparent inquiry into how learning, flexibility, diversity, governance, slack, trust, rigidity, and exposure shape response space under uncertainty.
