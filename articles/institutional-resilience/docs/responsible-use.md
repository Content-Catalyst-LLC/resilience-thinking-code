# Responsible Use

This scaffold is not a legitimacy-certification tool, public governance automation system, legal judgment tool, or institutional ranking system.

Do not use it to rank institutions without context, automate governance decisions, justify emergency overreach, certify public trust, replace legal or administrative judgment, weaken accountability, or hide unequal access and administrative burden behind a single resilience score.

Use it to make assumptions visible, compare strategies transparently, identify uncertainty, support learning, and strengthen accountable institutional resilience planning.
