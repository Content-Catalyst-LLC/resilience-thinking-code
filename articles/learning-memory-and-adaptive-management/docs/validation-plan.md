# Validation Plan

A professional learning, memory, and adaptive management workflow should include:

1. **Monitoring validation**  
   Confirm that indicators track meaningful system conditions, weak signals, slow variables, and distributional effects.

2. **Memory validation**  
   Review ecological memory, institutional records, technical documentation, community knowledge, staff continuity, and archival usability.

3. **Feedback-use validation**  
   Test whether monitoring and after-action reviews change rules, budgets, designs, staffing, thresholds, and practice.

4. **Forgetting-pressure review**  
   Identify turnover, political erasure, archive failure, outsourcing, ecological simplification, displacement, and loss of local knowledge.

5. **Governance validation**  
   Confirm that decision triggers, authority, escalation protocols, and accountability are strong enough to act on feedback.

6. **Justice review**  
   Assess whose memory is recorded, whose knowledge counts, who bears experimentation risk, and whether learning reduces unequal exposure.

7. **Implementation review**  
   Track whether lessons are assigned, funded, completed, and publicly reported.

8. **Uncertainty review**  
   Use scenario and Monte Carlo analysis to test robustness under uncertain learning, memory, governance, and disturbance assumptions.
