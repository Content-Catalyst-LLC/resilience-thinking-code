# Modeling Principles

This scaffold models future resilience strategy as a portfolio problem. Strategies are evaluated across adaptive capacity, buffering capacity, transformability, governance quality, equity performance, digital resilience, climate readiness, systemic exposure, and implementation burden.

The goal is not to identify a universal best strategy. The goal is to make assumptions visible, compare strategy rankings under different priorities, and test robustness under uncertainty.

Future resilience strategy should be evaluated by more than best-case performance. It should also be evaluated by exposure reduction, justice, governance capacity, transformability, digital fragility, climate readiness, and implementation feasibility.
