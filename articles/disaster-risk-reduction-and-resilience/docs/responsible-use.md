# Responsible Use

This scaffold is not a disaster safety certification, community ranking tool, or emergency-response replacement.

Do not use it to:

- claim that a community, infrastructure system, or disaster plan is safe
- rank vulnerable communities without context, participation, and accountability
- justify displacement, managed retreat, or infrastructure withdrawal without consent and justice safeguards
- hide uncertainty behind a polished score
- treat synthetic examples as empirical evidence
- ignore maladaptation risk
- reduce disaster justice to an optional variable
- replace engineering review, local knowledge, public health expertise, emergency management practice, ecological monitoring, or community governance

Use it to make assumptions visible, compare strategies transparently, identify uncertainty, support public learning, and strengthen accountable disaster risk governance.
