# Responsible Use

This scaffold is intended for synthetic-data analysis and professional modeling design. It should not be used as an automatic decision tool.

Do not use it to:

- rank ecosystems or communities without calibrated evidence
- justify disinvestment in systems labeled "low resilience"
- treat recovery speed as the only form of resilience
- ignore ecological thresholds or social vulnerability
- replace engineering review, ecological expertise, or public accountability

A strong resilience assessment asks: resilience of what, for whom, against what disturbance, and at whose cost?
