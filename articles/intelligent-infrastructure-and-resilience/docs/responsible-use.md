# Responsible Use

This scaffold is not an engineering safety certification, infrastructure rating, cyber compliance tool, procurement decision engine, emergency response system, surveillance system, or substitute for professional infrastructure assessment.

Do not use it to automate infrastructure investment, emergency prioritization, utility operations, safety-critical control, insurance decisions, policing, surveillance, or public-service access. Do not use aggregate scores to hide local service failure, environmental injustice, maintenance neglect, worker strain, data corruption, vendor lock-in, or cyber-physical exposure.

Use it to make assumptions visible, compare infrastructure resilience strategies transparently, identify hidden dependency and maintenance risk, support climate adaptation planning, strengthen public governance, protect vulnerable communities, and design intelligent infrastructure systems that remain accountable to people, place, ecology, and public purpose.
