# Validation Plan

A professional intelligent infrastructure resilience workflow should validate monitoring, predictive maintenance, cyber-physical security, digital twin capacity, redundancy, climate adaptation, governance, equity, ecological integration, fragility risk, and implementation feasibility.

Important validation sources include asset inventories, sensor calibration records, field inspections, engineering assessments, work-order histories, outage records, incident reports, climate exposure data, cyber/OT assessments, backup and failover tests, digital twin validation, utility service data, public health data, emergency management records, procurement contracts, vendor access logs, environmental justice data, community review, and after-action reports.

Quantitative indicators should be interpreted with engineers, operators, maintenance crews, cybersecurity teams, public works staff, emergency managers, data stewards, regulators, affected communities, and environmental justice stakeholders.
