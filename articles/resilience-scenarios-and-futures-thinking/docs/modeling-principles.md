# Modeling Principles

This scaffold treats resilience scenario practice as a system of horizon scanning, weak-signal detection, stress testing, adaptive pathways, participation, modeling, governance integration, equity sensitivity, and transformation potential.

Scenario value increases when a planning process expands anticipation, participation, governance learning, and decision relevance. It decreases when scenario design risk and implementation burden increase.

Future pathway simulation tracks system function, vulnerability, institutional learning, trust, adaptive buffer, transformation effect, fragility gap, equity-adjusted resilience, and robust resilience score over time.
