# Responsible Use

This scaffold is not a sustainability certification tool, impact-ranking system, development-aid allocation tool, or substitute for ecological science, community participation, or public governance.

Do not use it to certify that a project is sustainable, rank communities or countries without context, justify displacement, automate public investment decisions, weaken rights, or hide ecological overshoot and unequal vulnerability behind a single viability score.

Use it to make assumptions visible, compare pathways transparently, identify uncertainty, strengthen ecological and social safeguards, and support accountable sustainable resilience planning.
