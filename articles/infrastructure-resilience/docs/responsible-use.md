# Responsible Use

This scaffold is not an infrastructure safety certification, investment ranking tool, or replacement for engineering review.

Do not use it to:

- claim that an asset, utility, transport system, grid, water system, hospital network, or digital platform is safe
- rank communities without context, participation, and accountability
- justify service withdrawal or underinvestment
- hide unequal outage and recovery behind a single score
- treat synthetic examples as empirical evidence
- ignore cascading exposure or common-mode vulnerabilities
- reduce infrastructure equity to an optional variable
- replace engineering review, operator expertise, emergency management practice, cybersecurity assessment, ecological monitoring, or public governance

Use it to make assumptions visible, compare strategies transparently, identify uncertainty, support public learning, and strengthen accountable infrastructure resilience planning.
