# Responsible Use

This scaffold is not a community ranking tool, knowledge extraction tool, surveillance tool, Indigenous knowledge documentation system, public-decision automation engine, or substitute for consent and relationship building.

Do not use it to extract community knowledge, expose sensitive data, map sacred or vulnerable sites without consent, replace Indigenous governance, automate public decisions, rank communities, or hide unequal participation burden behind a single score.

Use it to make assumptions visible, compare strategies transparently, identify uncertainty, protect sensitive knowledge, strengthen participation access, improve decision accountability, and support responsible local-knowledge resilience practice.
