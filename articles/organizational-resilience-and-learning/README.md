# Organizational Resilience and Learning

Companion repository folder for **Organizational Resilience and Learning**.

This scaffold supports organizational resilience strategy comparison, weak-signal diagnostics, continuity and redundancy analysis, institutional-memory review, learning-stock simulation, workforce strain analysis, governance review, implementation-burden assessment, and uncertainty-aware resilience planning.

## Correct article directory

```text
articles/organizational-resilience-and-learning/
```

## Core modeling domains

- Anticipatory capacity and weak-signal detection
- Absorptive capacity, redundancy, and continuity
- Adaptive capacity and reconfiguration
- Organizational learning and after-action review
- Institutional memory and knowledge transfer
- Coordination, governance, and decision rights
- Psychological safety and worker voice
- Workforce protection, burnout risk, and ethical burden
- Implementation burden and feasibility

## Responsible-use note

The data are synthetic and the models are methodological examples. Real organizational resilience work requires worker participation, frontline knowledge, workload evidence, incident records, continuity-test results, psychological-safety review, governance review, privacy safeguards, and context-specific judgment.

Do not use this scaffold to surveil workers, rank teams punitively, justify understaffing, normalize burnout, automate personnel decisions, or replace participatory organizational learning.
