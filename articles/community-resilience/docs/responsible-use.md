# Responsible Use

This scaffold is not a community ranking tool, disaster-aid allocation tool, emergency certification tool, or substitute for community authority.

Do not use it to rank communities without local context, allocate aid automatically, justify managed retreat or abandonment, certify community safety, replace participatory planning, or hide unequal exposure and displacement risk behind a single resilience score.

Use it to make assumptions visible, compare strategies transparently, identify uncertainty, support learning, and strengthen accountable community resilience planning.
