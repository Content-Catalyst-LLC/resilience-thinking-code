# Validation Plan

A professional community resilience workflow should validate social capital, institutional capacity, infrastructure access, economic security, communication, public health, ecological condition, equity, and learning.

Important validation sources include participatory mapping, household surveys, social-network mapping, community-organization inventories, outage and service-access data, shelter and resilience-hub audits, local business and employment data, public-health data, environmental exposure data, aid-distribution data, recovery-timing data, and after-action implementation tracking.

Quantitative indicators should be interpreted with community knowledge, historical context, privacy safeguards, and attention to power. Vulnerability indicators should support protection and investment, not stigmatization.
