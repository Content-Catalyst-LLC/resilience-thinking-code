# Responsible Use

This scaffold is not an AI governance certification, safety case, emergency-management system, public-health system, climate risk model, lending model, surveillance tool, or substitute for participatory decision-making.

Do not use it to automate high-stakes decisions, allocate emergency resources without human review, target communities for surveillance, deny services, replace local knowledge, optimize away slack, or claim that synthetic scores represent real model safety, fairness, legality, or resilience.

Use it to make assumptions visible, compare AI-enabled resilience strategies transparently, identify model drift and AI-risk pathways, strengthen governance and human oversight, support participatory validation, protect vulnerable groups, and design AI systems that remain accountable to resilience, justice, and public purpose.
