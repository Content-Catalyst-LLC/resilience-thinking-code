# Responsible Use

This scaffold is not a procurement automation tool, supplier surveillance system, labor-risk scoring engine, or substitute for public governance, due diligence, stakeholder participation, and ethical review.

Do not use it to justify exploitation, hide labor or environmental harm behind resilience scores, expose confidential supplier information, automate procurement decisions without review, ignore workers and communities, or treat flow continuity as sufficient when risk is shifted onto vulnerable people.

Use it to make assumptions visible, compare strategies transparently, identify uncertainty, protect critical goods, strengthen public coordination, improve supplier resilience, protect labor rights, reduce cascading risk, and design more accountable supply chain resilience planning.
