# Validation Plan

A professional systems-resilience model should include:

1. **Boundary validation**  
   Confirm what system is being analyzed and whose risk is included.

2. **Feedback validation**  
   Test whether mapped feedback loops are supported by evidence rather than intuition alone.

3. **Delay validation**  
   Estimate actual detection, interpretation, response, and recovery delays.

4. **Stock-flow validation**  
   Confirm that stocks such as trust, biodiversity, savings, maintenance backlog, or institutional memory are measured appropriately.

5. **Threshold validation**  
   Distinguish known thresholds from hypothesized warning indicators.

6. **Leverage-point validation**  
   Assess whether proposed interventions change symptoms, buffers, information flows, rules, goals, or paradigms.

7. **Ethical review**  
   Ensure resilience language is not used to normalize burden shifting, abandonment, or harmful persistence.
