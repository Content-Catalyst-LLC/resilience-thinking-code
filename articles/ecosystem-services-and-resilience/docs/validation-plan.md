# Validation Plan

A professional ecosystem-service resilience model should include:

1. **Service definition validation**  
   Define the ecosystem service, beneficiary group, ecosystem source, spatial boundary, and temporal scale.

2. **Ecological validation**  
   Validate ecological condition, functional diversity, redundancy, threshold distance, and disturbance exposure with field data or defensible proxies.

3. **Service-flow validation**  
   Distinguish current service delivery from long-term service capacity.

4. **Bundle and tradeoff review**  
   Identify services that reinforce each other and services that compete under land-use, extraction, or infrastructure decisions.

5. **Equity and access review**  
   Determine who receives benefits, who bears costs, who decides, and whose knowledge is included.

6. **Scenario validation**  
   Test climate stress, land-use pressure, pollution, biodiversity loss, and governance failure scenarios.

7. **Uncertainty review**  
   Clarify which thresholds are observed, inferred, modeled, or uncertain.
