# Validation Plan

A real resilience-or-abandonment workflow should validate assumptions using disaggregated exposure data, infrastructure condition data, housing data, public health indicators, disaster aid records, insurance access, public service reliability, disability and language access review, community knowledge, local government fiscal capacity, relocation outcomes, public participation records, and after-action reports.

Quantitative scoring should be reviewed with affected residents, public agencies, community organizations, infrastructure operators, housing and health experts, legal advocates where relevant, environmental justice stakeholders, emergency managers, and governance practitioners.
