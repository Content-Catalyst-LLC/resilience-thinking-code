# Responsible Use

This scaffold is not a transition mandate, employment model, relocation model, resilience certification, investment directive, emergency decision tool, or substitute for public deliberation.

Use it to identify justice gaps, burden shifting, lock-in risks, transition support needs, ecological repair opportunities, and governance weaknesses. Do not use synthetic scores to justify displacement, deny resources, automate public decisions, rank communities, replace worker or community participation, or claim certainty about justice.
