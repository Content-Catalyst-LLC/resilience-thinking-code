# Validation Plan

A real just-transformation workflow should validate assumptions using disaggregated exposure data, public health data, housing and displacement data, labor market evidence, worker and community participation records, ecological indicators, infrastructure condition data, climate scenarios, public finance data, governance records, rights review where relevant, local knowledge, Indigenous and community knowledge where relevant, and after-action reviews.

Quantitative scoring should be reviewed with affected communities, workers, public agencies, ecologists, infrastructure operators, social protection experts, housing advocates where relevant, labor representatives where relevant, governance practitioners, and technical experts.
