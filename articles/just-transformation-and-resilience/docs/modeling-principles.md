# Modeling Principles

This scaffold models just transformation as the combination of structural change, resilience capacity, justice, ecological repair, governance legitimacy, livelihood protection, and exposure reduction.

Technical transformation alone is not enough. A pathway can reduce emissions, modernize infrastructure, relocate people, or restore ecosystems while still producing injustice if it shifts burdens, excludes affected communities, weakens livelihoods, or creates new lock-in.

The purpose of the scaffold is to make justice gaps visible alongside transformation performance.
