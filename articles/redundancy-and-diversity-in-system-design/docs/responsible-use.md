# Responsible Use

This scaffold is not a safety certification tool.

Do not use it to:

- claim a system is safe without stress testing and empirical validation
- reduce public protection because a model indicates redundancy
- rank communities, ecosystems, institutions, or infrastructure without contextual evidence
- ignore common-mode failure or maintenance quality
- treat justice as an optional score
- replace field inspection, ecological monitoring, engineering review, or community knowledge
- justify shifting risk onto workers, marginalized communities, ecosystems, or future generations

Use it to make assumptions visible, compare strategies transparently, identify hidden common-mode risks, and support accountable resilience design.
