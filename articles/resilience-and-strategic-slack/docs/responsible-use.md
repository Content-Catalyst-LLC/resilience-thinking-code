# Responsible Use

This scaffold is not a workforce surveillance tool, austerity justification, hoarding defense, procurement automation system, or substitute for participatory resilience planning.

Do not use it to punish teams for lacking capacity they were never funded to build, justify chronic overcapacity without purpose, normalize burnout, extract more labor from workers, exploit suppliers, hide deferred maintenance, or use aggregate scores to obscure shifted burden.

Use it to make assumptions visible, compare slack portfolios transparently, identify hidden fragility, protect people, strengthen continuity, preserve institutional memory, reduce cascading risk, and design accountable slack that supports resilience rather than privilege or waste.
