# Validation Plan

A professional strategic slack workflow should validate financial slack, workforce slack, operational slack, knowledge slack, network slack, governance slack, ethical safeguards, ethical burden, and implementation feasibility.

Important validation sources include budget and liquidity data, staffing and workload data, burnout and turnover indicators, worker voice, continuity exercise results, supplier and partner assessments, infrastructure maintenance records, backup test results, knowledge maps, decision records, after-action reviews, emergency authority protocols, public-capacity evidence, and stakeholder feedback.

Quantitative indicators should be interpreted with participatory review, worker and community voice, equity analysis, privacy safeguards, public accountability, and context-specific judgment.
