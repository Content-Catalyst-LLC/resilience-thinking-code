# Responsible Use

This scaffold is not a compliance engine, cybersecurity certification, safety case, vendor rating tool, or substitute for engineering review.

Do not use it to automate high-stakes security, compliance, safety, public-benefit, healthcare, infrastructure, or personnel decisions. Do not use aggregate resilience scores to hide user harm, worker burnout, accessibility failure, vendor lock-in, or technical debt inherited from underinvestment.

Use it to make assumptions visible, compare technology resilience strategies transparently, identify hidden dependency and technical-debt risk, support cyber recovery planning, strengthen data integrity, improve maintainability, protect users and workers, and design accountable public-interest technology systems.
