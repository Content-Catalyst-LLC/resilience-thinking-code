# Validation Plan

A real ethical resilience workflow should validate assumptions using disaggregated exposure data, recovery evidence, public participation records, community knowledge, local and Indigenous knowledge where relevant, disability access review, language access review, aid distribution records, relocation outcomes, affordability data, public health data, infrastructure service data, environmental justice evidence, legal analysis, and after-action reports.

Quantitative scoring should be reviewed with affected communities, public agencies, governance practitioners, social scientists, legal experts where relevant, environmental justice stakeholders, frontline workers, and technical experts.
