# Validation Plan

A professional transformation model should include:

1. **Regime diagnosis**  
   Define the current regime, the old system's stabilizing feedbacks, and why adaptation may be insufficient.

2. **Pathway validation**  
   Confirm that each pathway is plausible, legitimate, and meaningfully transformative rather than symbolic.

3. **Criteria validation**  
   Review criteria with domain experts, affected communities, and governance actors.

4. **Weight sensitivity**  
   Test whether rankings change under different ethical, ecological, governance, and risk priorities.

5. **Uncertainty validation**  
   Use Monte Carlo or scenario analysis to identify pathways that are robust under uncertainty.

6. **Justice review**  
   Assess who benefits, who bears costs, who has authority, and whether transition repairs or reproduces harm.

7. **Failure-mode review**  
   Test for elite capture, maladaptation, displacement, lock-in, resource failure, and loss of institutional memory.

8. **Governance review**  
   Confirm that model outputs connect to accountable decision-making rather than replacing public judgment.
