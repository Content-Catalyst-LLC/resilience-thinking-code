# Responsible Use

This scaffold is not a transformation mandate.

Do not use it to:

- impose relocation, austerity, institutional restructuring, or resource withdrawal
- rank communities or ecosystems without validated evidence and affected-community participation
- claim that one pathway is objectively best from synthetic data
- treat justice as a technical checkbox
- hide political choices behind model scores
- justify displacement, abandonment, or transition burdens for marginalized communities
- replace public deliberation, legal rights, local knowledge, or expert review

Use it to make assumptions visible, compare pathways transparently, identify uncertainty, and support accountable transformation planning.
