# Responsible Use

This scaffold is not a universal resilience scoring system.

Do not use it to:

- claim that one composite number proves a system is resilient
- rank communities or ecosystems without empirical evidence and participation
- justify underinvestment because a dashboard looks strong
- hide uncertainty inside weighted averages
- ignore threshold risk or early warning signals
- treat justice as optional
- replace local knowledge, domain expertise, stress testing, or public accountability

Use it to make measurement assumptions visible, compare metric frameworks transparently, identify hidden weaknesses, and connect resilience measurement to better decisions.
