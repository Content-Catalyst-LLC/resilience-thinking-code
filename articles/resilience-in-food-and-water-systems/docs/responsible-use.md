# Responsible Use

This scaffold is not a food security certification, water safety assessment, allocation tool, or substitute for professional judgment.

Do not use it to:

- certify drinking water safety
- certify food safety or nutrition adequacy
- rank vulnerable communities without participation and accountability
- justify water withdrawal, service exclusion, displacement, or denial of aid
- hide hunger, unsafe water, or unequal access behind aggregate scores
- treat synthetic examples as empirical evidence
- ignore Indigenous rights, local knowledge, customary water practices, or community governance
- replace hydrology, agronomy, public health, engineering, ecological monitoring, or food-security assessment

Use it to make assumptions visible, compare strategies transparently, identify uncertainty, support public learning, and strengthen accountable food-and-water resilience planning.
