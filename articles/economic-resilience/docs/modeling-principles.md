# Modeling Principles

This companion scaffold models economic resilience as a resistance, recovery, adaptation, transformation, equity, institutional-capacity, and implementation problem.

## Core variables

- resistance
- recovery
- adaptability
- transformability
- equity protection
- institutional capacity
- implementation burden
- sector concentration
- household fragility
- firm fragility
- financial exposure
- labor-market rigidity
- infrastructure exposure
- climate exposure
- fiscal pressure
- equity burden
- economic function under stress

## Core modeling logic

Economic resilience value rises when resistance, recovery, adaptability, transformability, equity protection, and institutional capacity increase. Value falls when implementation burden, demand shock, supply shock, financial stress, labor disruption, infrastructure stress, climate pressure, fiscal pressure, equity burden, and structural fragility increase.

The scaffold calculates strategy value, equity-adjusted value, institution-adjusted value, dynamic economic function under repeated stress, implementation-burden diagnostics, transformation pathway diagnostics, and Monte Carlo robustness under uncertainty.
