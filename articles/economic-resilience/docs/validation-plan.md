# Validation Plan

A professional economic resilience workflow should validate resistance, recovery, adaptation, transformation, equity outcomes, institutional capacity, and implementation feasibility.

Important validation sources include employment data, wage data, household income and debt data, firm closure and business formation data, credit access and default data, public finance records, sectoral concentration metrics, supply-chain dependency data, infrastructure service continuity data, insurance and climate exposure data, regional development data, social vulnerability data, and interviews with workers, firms, community institutions, public agencies, and affected regions.

Quantitative indicators should be interpreted with institutional history, public finance context, labor-market structure, community knowledge, climate risk, and distributional analysis.
