# Responsible Use

This scaffold is not a public-decision automation tool, regional ranking tool, austerity justification tool, or substitute for economic democracy, local knowledge, labor participation, and public accountability.

Do not use it to justify abandonment, rank regions without context, hide inequality behind aggregate resilience scores, replace democratic planning, ignore worker and community voice, or treat GDP recovery as sufficient evidence of resilience.

Use it to make assumptions visible, compare strategies transparently, identify uncertainty, strengthen public investment, protect vulnerable groups, support just transition, and design more accountable economic resilience planning.
