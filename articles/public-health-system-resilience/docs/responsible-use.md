# Responsible Use

This scaffold is not a clinical decision tool, public-health allocation tool, emergency triage tool, or certification of health-system safety.

Do not use it to triage patients, allocate scarce clinical resources, rank communities without accountability, certify public safety, replace epidemiological or clinical judgment, automate emergency powers, or hide unequal exposure and recovery behind a single resilience score.

Use it to make assumptions visible, compare strategies transparently, identify uncertainty, support learning, and strengthen accountable public health resilience planning.
