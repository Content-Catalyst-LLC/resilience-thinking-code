# Modeling Principles

This companion scaffold models small business and local economic resilience as a liquidity, workforce, supply, digital, public-capacity, community-wealth, equity, inequality-risk, and implementation problem.

## Core variables

- liquidity support
- workforce capacity
- supply resilience
- digital readiness
- public capacity
- community wealth
- equity access
- inequality risk
- implementation burden
- business function
- cash runway
- customer demand
- owner strain
- fragility gap
- adaptive capacity
- equity-adjusted function

## Core modeling logic

Local economic resilience value rises when liquidity support, workforce capacity, supply resilience, digital readiness, public capacity, community wealth, and equity access increase. Value falls when inequality risk, implementation burden, demand loss, supplier disruption, digital disruption, rent and debt pressure, workforce stress, insurance gaps, and public support delays increase.

The scaffold calculates strategy value, equity-adjusted value, business function under repeated shocks, cash runway, owner strain, fragility gap, recovery diagnostics, implementation-burden review, and Monte Carlo robustness under uncertainty.
