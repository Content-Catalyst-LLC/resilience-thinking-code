# Responsible Use

This scaffold is not a lending model, grant eligibility engine, procurement ranking tool, enforcement system, or substitute for local knowledge.

Do not use it to deny assistance, rank firms punitively, automate public support decisions, justify disinvestment, normalize owner burnout, obscure worker precarity, or claim that synthetic scores represent real firm viability.

Use it to make assumptions visible, compare resilience strategies transparently, identify hidden fragility, strengthen local support ecosystems, protect vulnerable firms, reduce unequal recovery, and design accountable small business and local economic resilience policy.
