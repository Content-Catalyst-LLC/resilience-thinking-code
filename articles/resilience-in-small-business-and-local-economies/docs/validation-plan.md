# Validation Plan

A professional small business and local economic resilience workflow should validate liquidity, workforce capacity, supply resilience, digital readiness, public capacity, community wealth, equity access, inequality risk, and implementation feasibility.

Important validation sources include business-owner interviews, worker input, local lending data, CDFI data, grant program records, procurement records, commercial rent data, insurance data, supplier data, business continuity plans, infrastructure data, broadband data, local disaster-risk maps, chambers of commerce, main street organizations, libraries, community colleges, municipal agencies, and neighborhood organizations.

Quantitative indicators should be interpreted with local knowledge, participatory review, equity analysis, privacy safeguards, and awareness of informal businesses, language barriers, administrative burden, and hidden owner or worker strain.
