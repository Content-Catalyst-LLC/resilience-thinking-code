# Responsible Use

This scaffold is not a climate safety certification, adaptation ranking tool, or relocation decision system.

Do not use it to:

- claim that a community, ecosystem, infrastructure network, or climate strategy is safe
- justify displacement, managed retreat, or infrastructure withdrawal without consent and justice safeguards
- rank vulnerable communities without context, participation, and accountability
- hide uncertainty or local knowledge behind model output
- treat synthetic examples as empirical evidence
- ignore maladaptation risk
- reduce climate justice to an optional variable
- replace climate projections, engineering review, ecological monitoring, public health expertise, or community governance

Use it to make assumptions visible, compare strategies transparently, identify uncertainty, support public learning, and strengthen accountable climate resilience planning.
