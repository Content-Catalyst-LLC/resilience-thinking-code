# Responsible Use

This scaffold is not a universal resilience dashboard or safety certification.

Do not use it to:

- claim exact resilience from a composite score
- rank communities without context, evidence, and participation
- hide uncertainty or missing data inside a polished dashboard
- treat absence of data as absence of risk
- average away threshold risk or justice red flags
- replace local knowledge, domain expertise, public accountability, or governance judgment
- use dashboard labels to justify underinvestment or delayed repair

Use it to make assumptions visible, compare dashboard designs transparently, identify red flags, strengthen uncertainty reporting, and connect indicators to accountable decisions.
