# Validation Plan

A professional financial system resilience workflow should validate capital strength, liquidity resilience, market infrastructure, governance capacity, inclusive resilience, systemic exposure, and implementation feasibility.

Important validation sources include supervisory balance-sheet data, regulatory capital data, liquidity coverage and funding data, counterparty and collateral data, payment and clearing data, market depth and volatility data, stress-test outputs, non-bank financial intermediation data, household debt and savings data, small-firm credit data, climate-risk exposure, cyber and operational-risk data, and institutional crisis-management evidence.

Quantitative indicators should be interpreted with legal authority, supervisory judgment, public accountability, institutional history, political economy, consumer-protection review, and distributional analysis.
