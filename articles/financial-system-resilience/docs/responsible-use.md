# Responsible Use

This scaffold is not a real-world supervisory model, investment model, credit model, institutional ranking tool, crisis-decision engine, or substitute for professional financial-stability analysis.

Do not use it to infer real institutional safety, automate regulatory action, make investment decisions, justify bailouts or austerity, obscure unequal financial harm behind aggregate metrics, or replace public accountability.

Use it to make assumptions visible, compare resilience strategies transparently, identify uncertainty, understand contagion pathways, strengthen payment and clearing continuity, protect households and small firms, and support more accountable financial resilience planning.
