# Responsible Use

This scaffold is not a resilience certification, transition mandate, investment model, emergency decision tool, infrastructure safety case, or substitute for public deliberation.

Use it to identify harmful persistence, lock-in, burden shifting, and transformation gaps. Do not use synthetic scores to justify displacement, deny resources, rank communities, automate policy choices, or claim certainty about maladaptive resilience. Real decisions require evidence, rights review, public accountability, and participation by affected people.
