# Validation Plan

A professional risk-governance and resilience model should include:

1. **Risk-frame validation**  
   Confirm system boundaries, affected groups, hazard scope, and time horizon.

2. **Indicator validation**  
   Verify that hazard, exposure, vulnerability, capacity, and governance indicators are supported by defensible evidence.

3. **Scenario validation**  
   Test moderate, repeated, compound, and slow-onset risk scenarios.

4. **Uncertainty review**  
   Distinguish measured values from proxies, assumptions, and expert judgments.

5. **Stakeholder review**  
   Include affected communities, frontline workers, domain experts, public agencies, and relevant civil-society groups.

6. **Equity review**  
   Determine whether resilience claims hide risk transfer, unequal exposure, or institutional abandonment.

7. **Governance review**  
   Assess whether model outputs lead to prevention, investment, adaptation, and accountability rather than blame.
