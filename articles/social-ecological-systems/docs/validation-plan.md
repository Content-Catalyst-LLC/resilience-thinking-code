# Validation Plan

A professional social-ecological systems model should include:

1. **Boundary validation**  
   Define the resource system, users, governance system, spatial extent, temporal scale, and external drivers.

2. **Ecological validation**  
   Use field evidence for ecological condition, regeneration, thresholds, disturbance regimes, and resource dynamics.

3. **Governance validation**  
   Assess rule legitimacy, monitoring, enforcement, accountability, conflict resolution, and adaptive capacity.

4. **Livelihood validation**  
   Evaluate resource dependency, income diversity, labor conditions, food security, and vulnerability.

5. **Knowledge-system review**  
   Integrate scientific evidence, local knowledge, institutional memory, and Indigenous knowledge where appropriate, with respect for rights and sovereignty.

6. **Scenario validation**  
   Test climate stress, market shocks, governance failure, infrastructure disruption, and transformation scenarios.

7. **Justice review**  
   Assess exposure, burden shifting, displacement risk, participation quality, benefit distribution, and accountability.
