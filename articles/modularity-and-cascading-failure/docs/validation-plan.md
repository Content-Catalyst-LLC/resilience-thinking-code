# Validation Plan

A professional modularity and cascade-risk workflow should include:

1. **Critical-function validation**  
   Identify the services, ecological functions, institutional functions, and community supports that must continue under disturbance.

2. **Dependency validation**  
   Map real dependencies across infrastructure, digital systems, ecosystems, institutions, suppliers, communities, and governance systems.

3. **Coupling validation**  
   Estimate how strongly and how quickly failures propagate across each dependency.

4. **Common-mode failure review**  
   Identify shared suppliers, platforms, fuels, regions, legal authorities, data systems, assumptions, and workforce dependencies.

5. **Isolation and containment validation**  
   Test whether failing modules can be separated without disabling the whole system.

6. **Coordination validation**  
   Review escalation pathways, mutual aid agreements, interoperability, data sharing, and public communication.

7. **Justice review**  
   Assess who lacks backup capacity, who is exposed to cascading failure first, and whether containment creates abandonment.

8. **Uncertainty review**  
   Use scenario and Monte Carlo analysis to test sensitivity to uncertain coupling, redundancy, and exposure assumptions.
